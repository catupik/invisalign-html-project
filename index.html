How does Invisalign treatment work?

These 3 steps can set you on the path to your brand new smile. Invis is an effective way to straighten teeth.




First step

Your first visit will be in the form of an obligation-free consultation where we’ll do a quick scan of your teeth.
This will give us a 3D visualisation of your teeth, which will show what your smile could look like once your teeth are straightened.
Using the iTero Element scanner, we can perform a quick and accurate 3D digital scan of your teeth to create an individualized treatment plan for you.







Second step

Begin the transformation of your smile from the moment you put on your aligners for the first time. This is a key step to achieving success. We will make sure your aligners fit well, answer your questions, and let you know what to expect.



Start wearing aligners

Start wearing aligners

When you go in for your first custom-made aligners, your Invisalign treatment provider will check your smile to make sure everything goes right from the start.

Regular appointments

Regular appointments

Approximately once every 6-8 weeks you come for a checkup. There the course of treatment will be checked, and at the same time pick up a new set of aligners.

As usual

As usual

Your Invisalign aligners are completely transparent and easy to remove, so you can eat and brush your teeth as usual.




How long does Invisalign treatment take?

Depending on the severity of your case , your treatment can be completed in as little as 6 months, with results starting to be seen within a few weeks.




Third step

Keep your brand new smile beautiful. Long-lasting results with our Vivera retainer. Vivera retainers are custom made and are 30% stronger than other retainers.
